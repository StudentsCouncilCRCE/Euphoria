COLLEGE DECOR


Participants: Only for the FEs

RULES:

1.No paints are allowed to be put on the walls of college Building.
2.Things used for Decoration should be should only be decorative Materials or creative material.
3.No class is allowed to decorate beyond the permitted area.
4.Cleanliness will be monitored throughout. It is very necessary to keep the area clean and tidy. If place is not tidy it could lead to Disqualification.
5.It is very necessary to finish the work a day before the Event.
6.Venue would be decided based on Chit system.
7.Hanging of the Decorative article is advisable.
8.Every class is responsible for removing the decorations after Euphoria.
