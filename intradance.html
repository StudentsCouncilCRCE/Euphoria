INTRA COLLEGIATE DANCE COMPETITON

JUDGING CRITERIA:

BLAZE IT:

1.All the classes have to send their class themes by 25th Jan. No changes in the theme after 1st Feb.
2.The competition will revolve around their individual dance themes.
3.All the tasks and advertising activities will be given from  30th Jan onwards, and it will be the responsibility of all the classes to have a track of all the activities given to them from the Instagram uploads on our official website: frcrce_official.
4.The protagonist will be your team contingent, and the activities should be taken in consideration to your themes.
6.All the classes  have to make their individual class instgram ID’s. (FE’s have to make one combined ID FECRCE_Euphoria19) The format is : BEIT_Euphoria19, TEELEX_Euphoria19
7.Each post uploaded on Instagram should have it’s comments disabled.
8.Tag @frcrce_official on every post you make, no other tags should be made, else that post will be disqualified.
9.The hashtag to be used on every post is #euphoriaiscoming_15. ( _ the last digits will change with the number of days left. )
10.Deadlines will be mentioned in all the posts. The uploads made after the deadline, will be disqualified.
11.No vulgarity should be displayed and control on foul language.
12.Rules for all the activities will be mentioned with the post.
13.The number of followers on your ID and the way each class maintains it’s feed plays an important role here.
14.The decisions and the winners of it will be completely based on the content of your post, unless mentioned.
15.Disrespecting any class or any faculty member would lead to disqualification.
16.The posts uploaded after the deadline won’t be considered and no points will be awarded.
17.The decision made by the judging committee is final.

DANCE PERFORMANCE:

  • Choice of music
  • Choreography
  • Costumes
  • Expression
  • Synchronization
  • Props
  • Theme
  • Stage coverage
