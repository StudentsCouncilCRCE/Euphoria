BIGBOSS


RULES:

Carry food and water, it will not be provided by us.
No watch or time keeping device will be allowed.
No electronic gadgets will be allowed in the big boss room.
No foul language should be used and no physical violence.
No Inter Department Pairing will be allowed unless and until specified if the task permits 2 players.
Eliminations will be done on basis of performance of tasks and votes.
No one can overrule Big Boss final decision.
Only 1 student per class will be allowed ie 16 participants in total. The class itself will select it’s representative for their respective class. 
ID card must be worn until you’re eliminated.
Only language to be used in the Big Boss house will be HINDI.
Further tasks will be revealed in the Big Boss House.
