Performing arts

  • Viral Voice(English) - Solo

    • If participant plays an accompaniment instrument then no additional setup time will be provided. Participants can have at the most one person for accompaniment. Participants using minus tracks have to submit the minus track pre-edited and with the 2.5 mins constraint. Use of abusive language will lead to disqualification.

  • Viral Voice(English) - Duet

    • Both the participants should be from the same branch/class. If participant plays an accompaniment instrument then no additional setup time will be provided. Participants can have at the most one person for accompaniment.Participants using minus tracks have to submit the minus track pre-edited and with the 2.5 mins constraint. Use of abusive language will lead to disqualification.

  • Dance face-off:

    • There will be two rounds and the final round will be the dance off. Maximum 6 entries per class will be allowed. ROUND 1: Prepare a routine. Dancers can apply for this in group/solo. The time limit for each performance/routine prepared is 5 mins. Dancers will be based on the basis of their technique, style and coordination. Top 6 will be selected for the dance-off. ROUND 2: A night before the event a list of 5-8 songs shall be given to the dancers. The song will be decided by drawing a chit. Dancers will be judged on their style and their sense of creativity.

Fun Events

  • Taboo

    • Taboo, the laugh out loud, out-of-your-seat game of forbidden words. In the Taboo game, you need to get your team to guess the secret word, but the obvious clues are strictly off-limits. To get your team to say pinball, would you say arcade, game, flippers, tilt or roll? In this game you can't because all of those words are strictly forbidden. Get your team to guess as many words as you can while you race against the clock. But watch out; if you say a forbidden word, the other team will "squeak" you and you lose the word.
